Welcome to ExpenseEase

ExpenseEase is your personal expense tracker. Manage your finances, set budgets, and generate reports with ease.

About ExpenseEase

ExpenseEase is an innovative web application designed to help you take control of your personal finances. With an intuitive interface and robust functionality, tracking and managing your expenses becomes effortless.

Features

  • Add, Edit, Delete Expenses: Seamlessly manage your expenses.
  • Categorize Expenses: Organize spending by categories for better insights.
  • Generate Reports: Create detailed reports to understand your financial habits.
  • Set Budgets: Monitor your spending against personalized budgets.

Technologies

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Django
  • Database: MySQL

Brief

ExpenseEase is an expense tracker that helps users manage their finances by categorizing expenses, setting budgets, and generating insightful reports. Whether you're managing personal expenses or handling small business finances, ExpenseEase gives you the tools to take control of your financial future.
